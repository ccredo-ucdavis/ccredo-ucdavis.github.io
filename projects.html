SkySpark HVAC Analytics & Workorder Tools

UC Davis Facilities Management Energy Engineering

SkySpark Axon HVAC Controls TRIRIGA Integration Data Analytics

During my internship with UC Davis Facilities Management Energy Engineering, I developed SkySpark-based tools to help review HVAC system behavior, diagnose building issues, and connect equipment data with maintenance workorders.

Technologies: SkySpark, Axon, TRIRIGA, Siemens Desigo, HVAC Controls, Building Automation Systems, Data Analytics

TRIRIGA Task Workorder Lookup

I created a view that connects building and equipment information with TRIRIGA workorder records. This allows users to quickly search task IDs, view task names, identify responsible organizations, and open linked maintenance records directly from SkySpark.

TherMOOstat Review Page

I developed a review page for zone-level HVAC diagnostics that displays relevant temperature, setpoint, airflow, damper, and workorder information in one view. This helps identify issues such as zones being too hot or too cold, sensor problems, and unusual discharge air temperature behavior.

Axon Development

The backend logic was written using Axon functions to query equipment, points, sites, and workorders. The code pulls relevant sensor points, organizes equipment metadata, and formats the results into usable review cards and tables.

Engineering Impact

These tools helped consolidate HVAC trend data, maintenance records, and equipment information into review pages that make troubleshooting faster and more organized.

Beam Vibration Analysis

Performed experimental vibration analysis using accelerometer measurements to identify natural frequencies, mode shapes, and measurement noise characteristics.

Valve Leakage Detection Project

Developed a fault-detection concept for HVAC heating and cooling valves by comparing valve command status against measured temperature changes across coils. The goal was to identify leaking valves before they caused occupant comfort issues or energy waste.

Technologies: HVAC Controls, Fault Detection, Temperature Analysis, Building Automation, Engineering Diagnostics
